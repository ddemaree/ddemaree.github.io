David Demaree

Softwaremaker

I'm a programmer, web designer, and businessperson, currently serving as senior product manager for Adobe Typekit, where I help lead user experience design and business strategy alongside a crackerjack team of done-getters.

Before joining Adobe, I worked on a small team building a local listings product for Metromix, a joint venture of Gannett & Tribune, and before that I ran a small agency that designed and developed Ruby on Rails apps for clients including DirecTV, Viacom, ABC, and T-26.

In 2016, A Book Apart published my first book, Git for Humans, a simple guide to Git and version control basics that aims to demystify what's really going on when you type Git commands. You can get the book directly from A Book Apart, or from iBooks. You can read an excerpt from the book, "The Art of the Commit", on A List Apart.

I live and work in the suburbs of New York City with my wife Jody Weinmann, and our daughter June, who is a lion.
